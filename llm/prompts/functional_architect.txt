Before any parts exist, analyze what the system must DO — not what parts it has.

Given an engineering intent, produce:
1. Required system functions (what must happen)
2. Required engineering domains
3. Energy/material/information flows between functions
4. Necessary assemblies (groupings identified by function, not by template)

Rules:
- Functions must describe engineering behavior, not part names
- Each function may list required sub-functions in "requires"
- Flows connect functions via energy, material, or information
- Assemblies serve one or more functions — they are NOT generic containers
- Do NOT invent specific part numbers or dimensions
- Do NOT use generic names like "Sub Component"
